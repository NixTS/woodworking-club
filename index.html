What we do & why we do it

Woodworking Workshops

Our social woodworking club offers diverse workshops and classes for all skill levels. Learn and improve your woodworking skills through hands-on guidance from our expert instructors.

Community Projects

Engage in meaningful community projects and collaborations. Contribute your woodworking expertise to create positive impact, from building structures to crafting furniture for charities.

Passion for Woodworking

We are driven by our passion for woodworking. Discover the artistry, precision, and creativity in this fulfilling craft that allows us to express ourselves through wood.

Building a Community

Connect with like-minded individuals in our inclusive woodworking community. Share knowledge, collaborate, and forge lasting friendships that inspire and motivate each other.

About Us

We are John, Sarah, and Mark, the founders of our social woodworking club. Our club brings together woodworking enthusiasts to learn, create, and socialize. We provide a space where like-minded individuals can explore joinery techniques, unleash creativity, and build lasting friendships. Sustainability is at the heart of what we do, as we promote eco-friendly practices and embrace sustainable materials. Join us on this fulfilling journey of craftsmanship, friendship, and personal growth. Experience the joy of woodworking and become part of our inclusive woodworking family.

Meet the Team

"Every piece of wood has a story to tell, and it's our job to bring it to life."

John, one of the founders of our woodworking club, brings a wealth of expertise in traditional joinery. He is dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of craftsmanship and guiding our members on their woodworking journey. John believes that woodworking allows individuals to connect with their creativity.

"In woodworking, there are no mistakes, only design opportunities waiting to be discovered."

Sarah is an integral part of our woodworking club, she has a keen eye for design and precision. She believes that woodworking is an art form that combines functionality and aesthetics. Sarah encourages members to embrace their unique style and explore their creativity.

"Woodworking is not just about creating beautiful objects; it's about leaving a positive mark on the world we live in."

Mark, a passionate advocate for eco-friendly woodworking practices. He encourages our members to explore sustainable materials, reduce waste, and embrace environmentally conscious techniques. Mark's commitment to making a positive impact on the environment shines through his craft.
