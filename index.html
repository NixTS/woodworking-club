What we do & why we do it

More than just woodworking.

At our club, we are passionate about woodworking and fostering a community of like-minded individuals who share the love for this craft. Whether you're an experienced woodworker or a beginner looking to learn, our club offers a welcoming and supportive environment for all skill levels.

We host regular meetups where members can gather in our well-equipped workshop to work on individual projects or participate in group activities. From carving and joinery to furniture making and woodturning, there's always something for everyone. Our experienced members are always ready to lend a helping hand, offer guidance, and share their expertise.

Through our club, we aim to create a supportive and inclusive space where individuals can explore their woodworking skills, learn from one another, and foster lasting friendships. We believe in the power of hands-on craftsmanship to inspire and bring joy to people's lives.

Beyond the individual benefits, our woodworking club also aims to give back to the community. We often undertake projects that contribute to local charities, schools, or other organizations in need. We believe in using our collective skills to make a positive impact and bring joy to others.

About Us

I am John, one of the founders of our social woodworking club. Alongside Sarah and Mark, we share a passion for woodworking and created a space for like-minded individuals to gather, learn, and create beautiful wooden masterpieces. With expertise in traditional joinery techniques, design, and eco-friendly practices, we have built an inclusive community where members can explore their craft, share knowledge, and forge lifelong friendships. Join us on this journey to experience the joy of craftsmanship and the warmth of our woodworking family.
